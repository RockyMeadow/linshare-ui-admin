Cross-domain communication rules

Inter-domain communication rules allows to manage (list / share) communication between users from different domains or within the same domain.
When you create a domain through the 'Domains Configuration' view and choose the 'Create option automatic ' for 'Cross-domain communication rules' , a rule will be created automatically by authorizing the newly created domain to communicate with itself only. All other communications are forbidden by default.
In order to validate a rule, it must be assigned to a domain.
