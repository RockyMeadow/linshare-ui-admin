Customize welcome messages

You can create several welcome messages per domain and associate them to sub-domains.
To do that :

  • In 'manage domains' menu, you can see a domain parent (readonly), your domains and subdomains (read/write).
    Then access to 'welcome messages configuration' and click on 'add button'.
    A pop-up will be displayed and you can fill in the blank a new welcome message's name.
  • Choose the duplicated message from the list, and you can edit its associated domain, content (for all LinShare available languages), description or name.
